Our Location More like our favourite surf spot

This acclaimed hotel is located in the quiet environs of Jayanagar in South Bangalore with the IT areas and shopping mall in proximity.

Local Attractions: Karnataka Golf Association: One of India's top & busiest courses the KGA course at Bengaluru is a hub for golf. With a challenging 18 hole, 7228 yard, Par 72 layout this course was redesigned in 2008 & now one of the toughest courses to play in the country. With water in play on 16 holes, Tifdwarf greens & a mature tree line its a course that can challenge the best. Estimated time: 5 minutes (200 mts)

Bangalore Turf Club Race Course: Covering 85 acres, the Bengaluru Turf Club Race Course is a tough track that requires horses to be fast and jockeys to be experienced. In addition to the main grass track, there are three training tracks, an equine swimming pool, walking rings, training schools, and more than 1000 stables. Estimated time : 40 minutes (10 kms)
